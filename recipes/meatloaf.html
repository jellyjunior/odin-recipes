Not My Meatloaf



Ingredients


  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespooks prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Directions

  1. Step 1
    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2
    In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, milk, bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste adn place in a lightly greased 9X5-inch loaf pan or form into a loaf and place in a lightly creased 9X13-inch baking dish.
  3. Step 3
    In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  4. Step 4
    Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
